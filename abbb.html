ITECCS

Institute of Technology, Engineering, Computing & Computational Sciences

ITECCS is a premier research institute advancing applied mathematics, artificial intelligence, scientific computing, and multiphysics simulation through rigorous theoretical foundations and scalable computational innovation. Our institute integrates mathematical modeling, high-performance computing, inverse methods, and AI-driven scientific discovery to address critical challenges across engineering, medicine, energy systems, and emerging technologies. ITECCS serves as a bridge between foundational scientific research and deployable real-world solutions—supporting universities, industry leaders, startups, and government agencies worldwide.

Message from the Director

Welcome to ITECCS — the Institute of Technology, Engineering, Computing, and Computational Sciences.

ITECCS was founded with a clear scientific mission and ambitious vision: to advance discovery, innovation, and technological leadership through the rigorous integration of engineering, applied mathematics, artificial intelligence, and high-performance scientific computing.

At ITECCS, we are committed to developing advanced mathematical models, scalable computational frameworks, and intelligent algorithms that enable predictive understanding and transformative engineering solutions.

Our institute serves as a collaborative platform connecting academia, industry, startups, and government partners worldwide.

As Director, I am committed to fostering an environment of scientific excellence, intellectual rigor, and meaningful collaboration.

Dr. Ahmed Kaffel
Founding Director
