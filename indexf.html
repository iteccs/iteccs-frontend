Institute for Theoretical & Computational Science (ITECCS)

A premier institute advancing applied mathematics, artificial intelligence, scientific computing, and high-performance simulation to address critical challenges across engineering, medicine, energy systems, and emerging technologies.

ITECCS integrates rigorous mathematical theory, scalable computational algorithms, and AI-driven methodologies to advance scientific discovery, accelerate technological innovation, and enable deployable solutions for academia, industry, and government.
