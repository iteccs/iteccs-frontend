Research & Innovation Programs

ITECCS advances the frontiers of applied mathematics, artificial intelligence, and scientific computing through the development of novel mathematical models, advanced numerical algorithms, and scalable computational technologies. Our research integrates rigorous theoretical foundations, physics-based modeling, and AI-driven methodologies to solve complex multidisciplinary problems across engineering, biomedical systems, energy technologies, and industrial innovation. These programs span foundational mathematical research, scientific algorithm development, computational physics, and industrial simulation, enabling transformative advances from fundamental discovery to real-world deployment.

Core Research Programs

Computational Imaging, Inverse Problems & Electromagnetics

  • Meshless imaging and inversion methods
  • Magnetic induction tomography (MIT)

  • Medical and biomedical imaging reconstruction
  • Scientific inverse problem solvers and regularization

Flagship Research Initiatives

Wavelet-Based Imaging and Inverse Modeling Systems

Development of advanced inverse modeling algorithms using wavelet-based and meshless numerical methods for biomedical imaging, computational electromagnetics, and scientific imaging systems.

Multiscale Porous Media Simulation for Industry and Energy

Rigorous mathematical and computational modeling of multiscale porous media transport systems, enabling industrial simulation, biomedical applications, and energy system optimization.

AI-Driven Scientific Computing and Digital Engineering Systems

Integration of AI, physics-based modeling, and high-performance computing to develop scalable digital twins and predictive engineering simulation platforms.
