About ITECCS

The Institute for Theoretical & Computational Science (ITECCS) is an advanced research, education, and innovation center dedicated to the development and application of high-impact mathematical, computational, and artificial intelligence methodologies.

ITECCS integrates rigorous theoretical foundations with high-performance scientific computing, multiphysics simulation, and data-driven modeling to address critical challenges across engineering, biomedical systems, energy, and emerging technological domains.

Institute Leadership and Vision

ITECCS operates at the intersection of mathematics, computational science, and engineering innovation, advancing next-generation technologies through research leadership and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Research and Technical Leadership

ITECCS leads interdisciplinary research programs spanning applied mathematics, scientific computing, artificial intelligence, and multiphysics simulation.

  • Advanced Mathematical Modeling and Scientific Computing for engineering, physics, and biomedical applications
  • High-Performance Computing and AI-Driven Simulation for predictive modeling and optimization
  • Computational Medicine and Biomedical Modeling supporting physiological modeling and medical imaging analysis
  • Multiscale and Multiphysics Simulation for energy systems, porous media, and advanced materials
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science for discovery, diagnostics, and predictive analytics
  • Industry Collaboration and Technology Translation transforming research into deployable solutions
