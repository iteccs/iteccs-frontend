Institute for Theoretical & Computational Science (ITECCS)

ITECCS is a premier research institute advancing applied mathematics, artificial intelligence, scientific computing, and multiphysics simulation through rigorous theoretical foundations and scalable computational innovation. Our institute integrates mathematical modeling, high-performance computing, inverse methods, and AI-driven scientific discovery to address critical challenges across engineering, medicine, energy systems, and emerging technologies. ITECCS serves as a bridge between foundational scientific research and deployable real-world solutions—supporting universities, industry leaders, startups, and government agencies worldwide.

Message from the Director

ITECCS was founded with a clear scientific mission: to advance discovery, innovation, and technological leadership through the integration of mathematics, computational science, and artificial intelligence. Our institute develops advanced mathematical models, high-fidelity simulations, and scalable computational frameworks that enable predictive understanding and transformative engineering solutions. Through interdisciplinary research, advanced education, and technical advisory, ITECCS empowers researchers, engineers, and institutions to translate scientific insight into real-world impact.
