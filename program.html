Research & Innovation Programs

ITECCS advances applied mathematics, AI-enhanced scientific computing, and scalable multiscale modeling to deliver predictive and deployable solutions across academia, federal research, and industry.

AI-Enhanced Scientific Computing & Inverse Modeling

Physics-informed AI (PINNs), deep learning for parameter estimation, inverse problems, PDE-constrained optimization, and uncertainty-aware prediction for engineering and scientific systems.

  • PINNs & parameter estimation
  • Inverse problems in imaging & materials
  • Uncertainty quantification & model reliability

Multiscale & Multiphysics Modeling

Coupled nonlinear PDE frameworks for complex systems spanning micro-to-macro dynamics in fluids, structures, porous media, reactive transport, and biomedical flows.

  • Porous media flow & swelling materials
  • Multiphase CFD & fluid–structure interaction
  • Biofluid dynamics & tissue modeling

High-Performance & Scalable Computing

Parallel algorithms and GPU acceleration for large-scale simulation, reduced-order models, and cloud-enabled HPC workflows supporting research and industrial deployment.

  • MPI/OpenMP/GPU acceleration
  • Reduced-order & surrogate modeling
  • Cloud-enabled HPC pipelines

Applied Mathematics & Numerical Methods

Advanced PDE modeling, FEM/FDM/spectral methods, stability and eigenvalue analysis, and gradient-based control for high-dimensional engineering simulations.

  • Higher-order FEM & spectral methods
  • Stability / eigenvalue analysis
  • PDE-constrained optimization & control

Digital Twins & Smart Engineering Systems

Digital twin architectures integrating simulation, IoT pipelines, surrogate optimization, and interactive visualization for rapid prototyping and decision support.

  • Virtual prototyping & rapid testing
  • Surrogate modeling for twin optimization
  • Visualization & interpretable analytics

Energy, Sustainability & Emerging Technologies

Computational modeling for energy systems, environmental transport, and sustainability—alongside exploratory work in quantum algorithms and nanoscale modeling when aligned with partner needs.

  • Battery & energy storage modeling
  • Environmental flows & water–energy nexus
  • AI for climate/resource optimization

Cross-Cutting Capabilities

  • Python, MATLAB, Fortran scientific computing
  • COMSOL, ANSYS, OpenFOAM multiphysics workflows
  • TensorFlow/PyTorch scientific AI pipelines
  • Visualization for analysis & decision-making
  • Secure cloud + DevOps for research applications

Collaboration & Engagement

ITECCS partners with federal agencies, academic institutions, startups, and established industry to deliver rigorous research, translational prototypes, and deployable computational tools.
